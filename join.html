Entry process

Club membership and season registration are not exactly the same thing, so players should join the club first and then follow organiser instructions for official season entry.

Who can join

Any student may join the SPKC International Chess Club on Chess.com.
Official season tournaments are only for registered season players.
Unregistered players must not join season tournaments, even if they are already club members.

How to enter the season

Step 1: Join the Chess.com club using the invite link above.
Step 2: Join the official SPKC Chess Club WhatsApp group — ask a current member or the organiser for the invite link.
Step 3: Fill in the online registration form with your full name, class, and Chess.com username.
Step 4: Wait for the organiser to review and accept your registration. You will be notified via the WhatsApp group once confirmed.
Step 5: You can check your registration status anytime on the Register page. You begin scoring season points from the first round after your registration is confirmed.

Important notes

Platform

Official club page

All official season tournaments are created inside the SPKC International Chess Club on Chess.com.

Schedule

Sunday 20:00 rounds

Players should be online and ready at least 5 minutes before the start time and should join the tournament lobby on time.

Late entry

Acceptance is not guaranteed

Late or mid-season applications may be accepted or rejected by the organiser depending on season management needs.
