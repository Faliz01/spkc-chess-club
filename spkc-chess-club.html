SPKC club identity

The club is open to students who want to learn, compete, and gradually build a stronger school chess culture with both casual and more serious players.

About the club

SPKC International Chess Club is the main hub for club activity and official online season tournaments on Chess.com.
The season follows a Swiss structure, with each round run as a separate Chess.com club tournament rather than a knock-out bracket.
This website keeps the key information in one place: club entry, schedule, standings, and rules access.
